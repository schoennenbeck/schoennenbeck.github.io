Database: S-Unit Groups of Orders

This is a joint project by Oliver Braun, Renaud Coulangeon, Gabriele Nebe and Sebastian Schönnenbeck. The database contains generators and presentations of the S-unit groups of certain orders in various types of simple rational algebras of small degree. The project uses our other project Database of unit groups.

  • Quaternion algebras over imaginary quadratic number fields
  • Indefinite quaternion algebras over the rationals
  • Rational division algebras of degree 3
  • S-arithmetic subgroups of exceptional groups
